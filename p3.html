Welcome to the ACM-W PCCOE Induction Program

We are thrilled to welcome you to the ACM-W PCCOE induction program! Our mission is to provide a platform for students to engage, learn, and excel in the field of computing and technology. This program is designed to foster a community of passionate and innovative minds.

The induction program includes a series of informative sessions, engaging workshops, and interactive events that aim to inspire and equip participants with valuable skills and knowledge.

As a part of ACM-W (Association for Computing Machinery's Women in Computing), we are committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity in technology, ensuring everyone gets an equal opportunity to thrive.

Program Details

The ACM-W PCCOE induction program features a diverse range of activities and sessions tailored to enrich the experience of every participant. Highlights include:

  • Interactive workshops on cutting-edge technologies like AI, Blockchain, and Web Development
  • Inspirational talks by industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and domain experts
  • Networking opportunities with professionals, alumni, and peers to grow your professional circle
  • Hands-on projects, coding challenges, and hackathons to test and enhance your skills
  • Career guidance sessions to help you map your future in tech

Our goal is to provide a comprehensive learning experience that encourages growth, collaboration, and innovation.

Speakers

Dr. Priya Ramesh - Chief Guest

Dr. Priya Ramesh is a distinguished professor and researcher specializing in artificial intelligence and machine learning. With over 15 years of experience, she has made significant contributions to the field and has been a keynote speaker at numerous international conferences.

Mr. Ankit Shah - Industry Expert

Mr. Ankit Shah is a senior software engineer with expertise in full-stack development. He has worked on large-scale projects in various domains and is passionate about sharing his knowledge and experiences with aspiring technologists.

Ms. Meera Desai - Entrepreneur & Mentor

Ms. Meera Desai, the founder of TechWomen Connect, is a leading voice in promoting women in STEM fields. She actively mentors young women entering the tech industry and has spoken at global forums on diversity and empowerment in tech.
